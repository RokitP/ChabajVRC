# ChabajVRC
In VRChat, no one actually reads bio's, (unless your a troller)
and so the most efficient way to get information through in
today's society has to be through constant outputs of info!
that is the goal of ChabajVRC. It has a simple, bare-bones way
to configure settings and get crucial information set up above
your head!

## How to use
1. edit your config.json, whether it'd be with nano, or vim!
2. Enter your IP, this is the internet IP of the client you'll
be displaying your Chat Badge on!
3. Fill out the rest of the information boxes, and they'll be
displayed above your head!
